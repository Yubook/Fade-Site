Fade is here to revolutionise the grooming industry and give barbers freedom. Set your own hours, your own rate, and your own policy. Get the app and apply to join Fade.

Your daily, weekly and monthly data at your fingerprints. Check your revenue live and get paid promptly.

Simply set your availability with a few clicks, super easy and in real time. You decide when you work and you don't.
